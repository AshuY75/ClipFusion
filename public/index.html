How to Convert Video into Shorts

  1. Select your long video.
  2. Enter the video width and final canvas height.
  3. Enter the video display height.
  4. Enter the title.
  5. Select duration.
  6. Click on "Process Video".
  7. Our system cuts it into short clips.
  8. Download your optimized Reels/Shorts.
  9. Post directly to Instagram or YouTube.
